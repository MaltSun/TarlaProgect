Raspberry Danish

A raspberry Danish is a delightful pastry that features a tender, flaky dough base, typically shaped into an oval or rectangular form. The dough is then filled with a luscious raspberry compote or jam, which adds a burst of sweet-tart flavor. Sometimes, a creamy cheese filling, such as cream cheese or almond paste, is layered beneath the raspberries, adding richness and depth to the pastry. After baking, the raspberry Danish is often drizzled with a sweet glaze or dusted with powdered sugar, enhancing its visual appeal and adding a touch of extra sweetness.

Composition

flour, butter, sugar, salt, yeast, eggs, milk, raspberry compote or jam, cream cheese or almond paste (optional), powdered sugar (for dusting, optional)

Price: kit of 6 - £13.50

Pain Aux Raisins

Pain aux raisins, also known as "raisin Danish" or "raisin swirl," is a delectable French pastry that showcases layers of buttery, flaky dough wrapped around a sweet filling of pastry cream and plump raisins. The dough is typically rolled out thinly, spread with pastry cream, and sprinkled with raisins before being rolled into a spiral shape. After baking, the pain aux raisins emerges from the oven with a golden-brown crust and a tantalizing aroma that hints at its indulgent interior. Often, a light glaze or dusting of powdered sugar is added to enhance its sweetness and visual appeal.

Composition

flour, butter, sugar, salt, yeast, eggs, milk, pastry cream, raisins, powdered sugar (for dusting, optional)

Price: kit of 6 - £13.50

Apricot Danish

An apricot Danish is a delightful pastry that combines tender, flaky layers of dough with a sweet and tangy apricot filling. The dough is typically shaped into a square or rectangular form and then filled with apricot jam or compote, which lends a burst of fruity flavor to every bite. After baking, the apricot Danish is often adorned with a light glaze or dusting of powdered sugar, enhancing its sweetness and visual appeal.

Pain Au Chocolat

Pain au chocolat, also known as a chocolate croissant, is a delectable pastry that combines the buttery, flaky layers of a croissant with rich, indulgent chocolate filling. The pastry dough is rolled out thinly and then filled with bars or squares of high-quality dark chocolate. The dough is then folded over the chocolate, creating multiple layers of pastry enveloping the chocolate filling. After baking, the pain au chocolat emerges from the oven with a golden-brown exterior and a melty, chocolatey interior. The combination of crispy, buttery layers and decadent chocolate makes it a beloved treat for breakfast or any time of the day.

Composition

flour, butter, water, milk, sugar, salt, yeast, dark chocolate

Price: kit of 6 - £13.50
