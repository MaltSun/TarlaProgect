How to make an order

Explore Our Selection

Begin by perusing our delectable assortment of pastry kits on our user-friendly website. From classic croissants to indulgent eclairs, we have something to satisfy every craving.

Add to Cart

Once you've identified the irresistible treats you'd like to indulge in, simply add them to your virtual shopping cart with a click. Take your time to ensure you've selected all your favorites.

Proceed to Checkout

When you're ready to complete your order, proceed to our secure checkout. Here, you'll enter your delivery details and any special instructions. Rest assured, our checkout process is quick and hassle-free.

Confirmation and Preparation

After placing your order, you'll receive a confirmation email detailing your purchase. Our skilled team will then spring into action, meticulously preparing your pastry kits with the utmost care.
