Our message

Customer Health and Well-being First

We are committed to producing products that promote the healthy lifestyles of our customers. All our baking mixes are guaranteed gluten-free and sugar-free, providing optimal dietary solutions for everyone, regardless of their dietary needs.

Preserving Nature for Future Generations

We are dedicated to an environmentally responsible approach in everything we do. Our products are not only safe for consumption but also crafted with environmental concerns in mind. We use packaging that is easily biodegradable and recyclable to reduce our footprint on the planet.

Uncompromising Quality

We strive for the highest standards of quality in all aspects of our business. Our products undergo rigorous selection and quality control to ensure that every baking mix bearing the Tarla brand meets our high standards of taste, safety, and ethics.

How to make an order

Explore Our Selection

Begin by perusing our delectable assortment of pastry kits on our user-friendly website. From classic croissants to indulgent eclairs, we have something to satisfy every craving.

Add to Cart

Once you've identified the irresistible treats you'd like to indulge in, simply add them to your virtual shopping cart with a click. Take your time to ensure you've selected all your favorites.

Proceed to Checkout

When you're ready to complete your order, proceed to our secure checkout. Here, you'll enter your delivery details and any special instructions. Rest assured, our checkout process is quick and hassle-free.

Confirmation and Preparation

After placing your order, you'll receive a confirmation email detailing your purchase. Our skilled team will then spring into action, meticulously preparing your pastry kits with the utmost care.
